Instructions
  • Press "Challenge" to fight with AI
  • Press "Show AI" to run AI automatically
  • Press "Reload" to reload the game. You need to reload each time when you finish a game.
  • You can choose the difficuly of game, and the speed of show through two drop-down menus.
  • Press "UP" on keyboard to rotate the shape.
  • Press "LEFT" and "RIGHT" on keyboard to move the shape horizontally.
  • Press "DOWN" on keyboard to finish a move.
  • You can also use the virtual keyboard to control.
